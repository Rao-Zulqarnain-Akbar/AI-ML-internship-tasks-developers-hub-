Key Insights
📐
Area is the Strongest Predictor
Square footage accounts for 47.2% of feature importance in Gradient Boosting, and has a 0.54 correlation with price — the highest of all features.
🏆
Linear Regression Wins
Despite its simplicity, Linear Regression achieves the best R² of 0.649 on the test set — suggesting the price-feature relationships are largely linear.
🚿
Bathrooms > Bedrooms
Bathrooms have 3.4× the feature importance of bedrooms (16.7% vs 4.9%) and a stronger price correlation (0.52 vs 0.37).
❄️
AC is a Premium Signal
Air conditioning is the 3rd most important feature (7%), with a 0.45 correlation with price — it signals higher-end properties.
📊
Skewed Price Distribution
Mean price (₹47.7L) exceeds median (₹43.4L) indicating a right-skewed distribution — a few luxury homes pull the average up significantly.
🛋️
Furnished Homes Cost Less?
Furnishing status has a −0.31 correlation with price — unfurnished homes command higher prices, likely because they're larger or in better locations.
